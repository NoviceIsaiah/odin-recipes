Green Bean Casserole

Description

Green Bean Casserole: Traditionally a side dish made with a bowl of green beans covered in cheese and breading. This creates a hard top layer with a creamy inside that makes for a wonderful Thanksgiving dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of green beans
  • 1 Can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 Cup shredded Cheddar chesse
  • 1 6oz can French-fried onions
  • Optional: Half slab of salted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix green beans and condesed soup together into microwave-safe bowl
  3. Microwave bowl until warm
  4. Stir 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese into the green bean mixture. Microwave for 2-3 minutes
  5. Transfer mix to a casserole dish. Spread even over the bottom and add remaining Cheddar over top. Then sprinkle with French-fried onions
  6. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and onions just turning brown. About 10 minutes
